Haze

Beautiful, Accurate Weather

A calm, editorial weather app for iPhone — big serif numerals, soft skies that shift with the day, and accurate forecasts. No ads. No accounts.

Every screen moves with the sky: the background drifts from dawn to day to dusk to night, tinted by the current conditions, with a soft atmospheric haze along the horizon. Data comes from Open-Meteo — a blend of the ECMWF, GFS, and ICON models — with nothing to clutter the view.

  • A living sky that matches the exact moment, dawn to night
  • Current conditions with feels-like and daily high & low
  • Hourly forecast and a 72-hour trend you can scrub
  • 10-day outlook with hour-by-hour detail
  • A sunrise & sunset arc you can drag across the day
  • Wind, UV, humidity, pressure, cloud cover & dew point
  • US air quality and precipitation
  • A home-screen widget that refreshes on its own
  • Saved locations and your current location
  • Switch between °F/°C and mph/km·h
