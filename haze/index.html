Haze

Beautiful, Accurate Weather

Haze is a weather app for iPhone that's actually nice to look at. The forecasts are accurate, the design stays out of the way, and there are no ads or accounts.

The background shifts with the time of day and the current conditions, so the app looks different at dawn than it does at night. Forecasts come from Open-Meteo, which blends the ECMWF, GFS, and ICON models. Nothing else gets in the way.

  • A sky that changes with the time of day
  • Current conditions, feels-like, and the day's high and low
  • Hourly forecast and a 72-hour trend you can scrub
  • 10-day outlook with hour-by-hour detail
  • A sunrise and sunset arc you can drag across the day
  • Wind, UV, humidity, pressure, cloud cover, and dew point
  • US air quality and precipitation
  • A home-screen widget that refreshes on its own
  • Saved locations and your current location
  • Switch between °F and °C, mph and km/h
